Summary: Latitude vs. X

For this assignment we wanted to see how different aspects of weather change as you get closer to the equator. We pulled weather data from the OpenWeatherMap API, brought the data into a pandas dataframe, and used MatPlotLib to plot the data comparing a variety of different conditions. We compared distance to the equator with max temperature, humidiity, cloudiness and wind speed. This site includes the individual visualization graphs for each of these four comparisons, a comparison page, as well as a data tab with a table of the data used in this project.
